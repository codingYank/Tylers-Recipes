Pork Souvlaki

Ingredients

  • 28 oz Pork Tenderloion
  • 100 ml Olive Oil
  • 1 tbsp Vinegar
  • 1 tbsp Dried Oregano
  • 1 tsp Honey
  • Salt and Pepper
  • 8 Skewers
  • 8 Pita Breads

Ingredients (For Sauce)

  • 1 Cucumber
  • 2 Cloves Garlic, Minced
  • 1/4 cup Olive Oil
  • 18 oz Plain Yogurt
  • 1-2 tbsp Red Wine Vinegar
  • Salt and Pepper to taste

Instructions

  • Cut pork into bite sized pieces and place in a bowl. Add all the other ingrediants and mix to ensure pork is covered. Allow to marinate for 20 minutes
  • In a large bowl grate cucumber and season with salt and pepper, allow to site for 10 minutes. Then wrap cucumber in a kitchen towel and squeeze to get rid of most of the water. Into a bowl add the cucumber and remaining sauce ingredients. Mix well and add salt and pepper to taste.
  • Put the pork onto the skewers. Cook on a grill or in a grill pan over high heat for about 10 minutes, turning occasionally.
  • Prepare the pita bread by brushing each side with olive oil and seasoning with salt and oregano. Place the pitas in an oven preheated to 482 degrees for 2 - 3 minutes.
  • To serve remove the pork from the skewers and put an a pita and top with some of the sauce and a squirt od fresh lemon.
